About Me

Hi, I'm Jasper. I'm a teenage musician and software developer living in Sydney, Australia. I love what I do and think that more people need to get into the industry and start to work on game design and development. I currently run a Game Development & Design Studio and a Alt Rock Record Label called Sulfuric Studio. We have also started a public Minecraft Server with a focus on fun and creativity.
